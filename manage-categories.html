Manage Categories

Saved!

Click the pencil on any row to edit its label, ID, icon, or colour. Drag rows to reorder. Click the trash to remove (only if no notes use that category).

How This Works

Categories appear as the top-level groupings for your notes (Power Apps, Power Automate, etc.).

Click the pencil icon to rename a category or change its icon/colour inline. Changes are only saved when you click Save to GitHub.

To remove a category, all notes in that category must first be moved or deleted.

After saving, refresh the new-note and edit-note forms to see the updated category list.

Available Icons
Click the icon button on any row to pick a new icon.
